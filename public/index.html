Our mission

We at teachmehowtocode believe that it is everyones right to learn coding in modern and relevant programming languages, like go, rust, python, javascript, and their other friends.

How are we going to achieve that?

We strongly believe in the DIY mantra. Coding cannot be taught on whiteboards nor should it be. To learn coding you must code plain and simple.

In the upcoming weeks we will introduce you to a brilliant hands-on workflow that will enable you to work on project-based problems and get immediate feedback.
